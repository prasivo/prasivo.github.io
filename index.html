Welcome to PRASIVO

Pharmacy education ko dekhne ka nazariya badalne wala platform. Humne ise student ki un pareshaniyon ko dekh kar banaya hai jo 10 alag jagah dhoondte hain aur end me confuse ho jate hain.

What is PRASIVO?

Prasivo ek system hai. Ye sirf notes nahi deta, balki aapko "Learning Layers" provide karta hai. Jab aapko pass hona ho toh Pass Layer padhein, jab deep janna ho toh Power Layer, aur jab topper banna ho toh Elevation Layer.

Why PRASIVO is different?

Baki platforms par sirf PDFs ki bheed hai. Prasivo par Logic hai. Hum batate hain ki Examiner kya soch raha hai aur marks kahan milte hain. Humara focus pure high-end content par hai.

Student ka kya fayda hoga?

Aapko kisi heavy hardware ki zaroorat nahi. Saara content optimized hai. Topic samajhne ke liye logical diagrams aur easy language (Hinglish) ka use kiya gaya hai taaki cheezein ratni na padein.
