What is PRASIVO?

Prasivo ek system hai. Ye sirf notes nahi deta, balki aapko "Learning Layers" provide karta hai. Jab aapko pass hona ho toh Pass Layer padhein, jab deep janna ho toh Power Layer, aur jab topper banna ho toh Elevation Layer.
