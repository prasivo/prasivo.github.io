About Prasivo

Prasivo is an all-in-one smart learning platform designed for pharmacy students. It focuses on clear concepts, exam-oriented study material, and a clean learning experience.

Why Prasivo?

Most students struggle by switching between multiple books, PDFs, apps, and websites. Prasivo aims to solve this by bringing everything into one structured and reliable platform:

  • Official syllabus reference
  • Subject-wise study flow
  • Important and repeated exam questions
  • Smart learning with AI assistance (planned)
