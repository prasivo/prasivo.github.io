Human Anatomy and Physiology

Human Anatomy and Physiology deals with the structural organization of the human body and the functions of its various systems. This subject forms the foundation for understanding health, disease, and pharmaceutical sciences.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic anatomical terminology
  • Levels of structural organization
  • Cell structure and functions
  • Human tissues and their types
  • Major organ systems of the body

Major Body Systems

  • Skeletal System
  • Muscular System
  • Cardiovascular System
  • Respiratory System
  • Digestive System
  • Nervous System
  • Endocrine System
  • Urinary and Reproductive Systems

Importance in Pharmacy

Understanding human anatomy and physiology is essential for comprehending drug actions, disease mechanisms, and therapeutic interventions in pharmaceutical practice.
