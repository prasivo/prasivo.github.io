# Prasivo

Prasivo is an all-in-one smart learning platform designed for pharmacy students.
It focuses on clear concepts, exam-oriented study material, and a clean learning experience
for Semester exams, GPAT, CUET-PG, D.Pharm, and B.Pharm students.

---

## Why Prasivo?

Most students struggle by switching between multiple books, PDFs, apps, and websites.
Prasivo aims to solve this by bringing everything into one structured and reliable platform:

- Official syllabus reference
- Subject-wise study flow
- Important and repeated exam questions
- Smart learning with AI assistance (planned)

The goal is simple: **study from one place without confusion.**

---

## Features

### Current
- Clean and responsive homepage
- Working navigation menu
- Notes section structured around the PCI syllabus
- Subject-wise organization
- Mobile-friendly design

### Planned / In Progress
- Full study material for each subject
- 2, 5, and 10 marks important & repeated questions
- AI-based explanation mode (Hinglish, exam + concept focused)
- GPAT and CUET-PG focused learning paths
- Quick revision and exam-oriented sections
